Crystal Collector



Use invisible random numbers to match the computer's randomly chosen number!

How to play

  • 1. The computer has generated five random numbers. One that you can see below and four that are hidden in the crystals.
  • 2. Use the crystals to match the computer's number. Clicking on the crystals will reveal it's secret value and add points to your crystal score.
  • 3. You can win the game by matching your crystal score to the computer's random number. You'll lose the game if your crystal score is greater than the random number.
  • 4. After each game, all the random values will be reset.
